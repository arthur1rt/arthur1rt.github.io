Square x Square

Square x Square is currently on Steam. It is a local PvP game (up to 4 players) which your objective is to survive the battle. You may choose a helmet, a weapon and a perk to battle against your friends (or enemies). On the stage, a chest is randomly generated. The player who collects the chest will trigger a global random event.

Jack in the maze

Jack in the maze was a game developed to study random generated map and real-time minimap.
It is a survivor-like game, which you have to clear all the stage to get to a new one. Moreover, you can get upgrades on your abilities and increase your stats. The objective of the game is to survive as long as possible.

MonSTEROID

MonSTEROID is a game developed on the LudumDare#33 GameJam.The theme was “You are the Monster”.
We decided to play with the theme and tell a story of a boy who becomes a muscle monster using steroids to beat up the bullies of his school.
Your objective is to use your skills to beat the final boss.

LudumDare

Skembie

Skembie is a one-week game made to simulate a game jam.
Your objective is to survive as long as possible possible. Touch the screen on the right time to shoot and kill the enemies. If you miss the right time to shoot, your bullets will deal less damage (or not even deal damage).

Math Master

Math Master was a final project for my Digital Games Production course.
Math Master is a RPG turn-based game that encourages the fast thinking to solve basic math problems. Also, it is an example of how fun games can be created applying gamification.
